Overnight Blueberry Oats

Oatmeal is nutritious breakfast meal that can be easily prepared ahead of time which makes it perfect for busy individuals. Using this recipe as a baseline, you may want to mix and match your own ingredients to modify the textures and flavours to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons chia seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon or more to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh blueberries

Directions

  1. Combine all the ingredients except the fruit in a jar, seal it and shake well
  2. Mix in the blueberries (or your fruit of choice)
  3. Seal the jar and refrigerate overnight
